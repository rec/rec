# Lovely Numbers: Math made better for almost everyone

This book is written for almost anyone who might need or like to know a bit about
mathematics but I am most interested in regular people who have a bit of trouble with
math, who find sums a bit daunting, who have unhappy memories of math class.

The trouble a lot of the time is mathematics classes themselves, which rush
kids through the explanations and understanding, to get to the grinding the crank
of adding, multiplying, fractions, and all that, to get answers.

But it took thousands of years between the time humans started using numbers before we
finally understood what a number really was.
